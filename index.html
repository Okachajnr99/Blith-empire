About Terracore Spatial Consultants

Terracore Spatial Consultants Ltd is a professional land and geospatial surveying firm dedicated to delivering innovative, precise, and efficient spatial solutions across Kenya and beyond. We combine advanced surveying technologies with extensive field experience to provide clients with accurate data for informed decision-making.

Our team comprises highly skilled professionals with expertise in land surveying, mapping, civil works, GIS, and drone technology. We pride ourselves on integrity, professionalism, and commitment to excellence in every project we undertake.

Our Vision

To be a leading provider of geospatial and land surveying solutions through innovation, technology, and professionalism.

Our Mission

To deliver accurate, reliable, and technology-driven spatial data that empowers infrastructure development and land management.
